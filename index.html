What this is

A standalone, offline demonstration of the Advisor Digital Twin. It captures how a retiring advisor runs their book, turns it into a portable Methodology Profile, and uses that profile to drive the incoming advisor during and after the handoff. It runs entirely in your browser and ships with a synthetic book so you can click through immediately, or upload your own CSVs in Ingest.

2 · Discover the methodology

The engine reads the action log and mines recurring condition → action patterns, scoped by segment, each with a confidence score derived from how consistently it appears. This is the raw methodology, before the advisor confirms anything.

3 · Elicit what the data cannot show

The engine flags low-confidence rules to confirm and gaps to fill. Answering these raises confidence, upgrades provenance to observed + elicited, and captures judgment notes.

Run the extraction engine first (step 2), then return here.

4 · The Methodology Profile

One versioned object: segmentation, the decision rulebook, communication cadence, judgment notes, and a gap register. This is the portable artifact you would hand to a successor or store as a continuity record.

5 · Activate: drive the successor

The same profile powers the handoff. During the overlap it produces a work queue and per-household playbooks; in shadow mode the successor makes calls and the Twin scores adherence to the predecessor's method.
