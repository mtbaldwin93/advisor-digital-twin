2 · AI Capture Simulated AI

This is where the AI does the work: it reads your data, asks a few sharp questions, and turns the answers into a documented methodology, the rules, the judgment, and the voice.

Step A · Read the data
The AI scans everything you uploaded and extracts the advisor's rules and cadence.
Step B · Answer a few questions
The AI asks what the data can't show, your judgment and the exceptions. Plain English, about two minutes.
Step C · Generate the insight brief
A plain-English read on the advisor's methodology, business acumen, and blind spots.
Step D · Draft in their voice
A client note written in the advisor's style, for the successor to reuse.
Advanced: connect the real Claude (optional)
