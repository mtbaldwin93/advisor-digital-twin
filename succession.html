Questions

Everything you're wondering

Do I need to be technical to use this?+

No. You upload files and answer a few plain-English questions. The AI does the heavy lifting, and everything it produces is reviewed and approved by you. If you can send an email, you can build your Twin.

What happens to my clients' data?+

It's processed to capture your methodology, then discarded. We store the derived playbook and its history, not your client records. Wallace takes no custody and no discretion, and you can export or delete your Twin at any time.

How long does it take to get started?+

About ten minutes to begin. You'll have a first draft of your Methodology Profile the same session, then refine it over time as your practice evolves.

What if my succession is years away, or happens suddenly?+

That's exactly why it's a living twin. It stays current as you work, so whether you're planning a handoff for 2030 or an unexpected event happens tomorrow, your successor inherits a complete, up-to-date playbook.

Does this replace my advisor or my judgment?+

Never. It captures your judgment so it can be carried forward by the person who takes your place. You stay the decision-maker; the Twin makes your decisions transferable.
